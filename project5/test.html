If you found a stray dog, you've come to the right place.

At Syracuse Stray Compass we strive to ensure that no dog is left to fend for itself and hope to give them the better life that they deserve.

Syracuse Stray Compass is here for you!

The Syracuse Stray Compass helps the citizens of Syracuse and the surrounding areas responsibly manage stray dogs that are found on the streets. All too many times a stray dog ends up in a cycle of being rehomed and returning to the streets because of irresponsible rehoming groups on social media. While intentions are to save the strays from ending up in a kill shelter, they often give away these found strays to the first person who offers to take them, without ensuring that they have a safe home or the right intentions for these dogs. The results may vary, but all too often these dogs end up in dog fighting rings or back on the streets.


These repercussions can be avoided by educating the public and by streamlining the process of rescuing a stray dog. There are a number of projects and resources in the greater Syracuse area, but unfortunately, their websites are outdated and lead to dead ends. By streamlining the process of reporting and rehoming a stray, more dogs will be able to find a safe and loving forever home.
